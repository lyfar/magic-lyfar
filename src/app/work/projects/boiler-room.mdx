## Project Overview

We were entrusted by Boiler Room to produce their official Instagram Stories video content for their Hong Kong event. Our team focused on creating dynamic, real-time video stories that captured the authentic spirit of Hong Kong's underground electronic music scene.

### Our Role

- **Video Stories Production**: Created and managed real-time video story content throughout the event
- **Content Strategy**: Developed a narrative structure for the evening's video coverage
- **Audience Engagement**: Maintained Boiler Room's signature raw, unfiltered aesthetic while ensuring maximum engagement
- **Real-time Editing**: On-site video processing and publishing to maintain immediacy

### Impact

The Instagram Stories reached over 2 million viewers globally, with an exceptional completion rate that exceeded platform averages. The project demonstrated our ability to work within established brand guidelines while delivering engaging, time-sensitive video content for major music industry clients.
