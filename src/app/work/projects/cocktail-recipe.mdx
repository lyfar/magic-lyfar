## Project Brief

We produced an elegant cocktail recipe video that transforms a simple drink preparation into a visual feast. The project highlighted premium ingredients and professional techniques while maintaining an accessible, engaging format.

## Approach

**Macro Cinematography**: Captured detailed shots of ingredients and mixing techniques.
- **Color Palette**: Used rich, sophisticated colors to enhance the premium feel.
- **Step-by-Step Flow**: Structured the content for clear, easy-to-follow instructions.

## Result

A sophisticated recipe video that elevates the cocktail-making experience and showcases the artistry of mixology.
