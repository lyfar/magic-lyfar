## Project Brief

We partnered with Ermano Lelli, an Italian chef and entrepreneur who established multiple culinary ventures in Hong Kong, to create an educational cooking series. Filming took place in his private kitchen, focusing on authentic Italian pasta preparation and sauce techniques.

## Approach

- **Intimate Kitchen Setting**: Utilized Ermano's private kitchen to create an authentic, personal cooking environment.
- **Professional Production**: Complete pre-production planning, professional lighting setup, and voice recording.
- **Educational Focus**: Structured filming to capture clear instructional content for pasta and sauce preparation.
- **Series Format**: Designed as part of a larger cooking series showcasing Italian culinary traditions.

## Result

A high-quality instructional video that captures Ermano's expertise and passion for Italian cuisine, providing viewers with authentic techniques for pasta preparation in an intimate, professional setting.
