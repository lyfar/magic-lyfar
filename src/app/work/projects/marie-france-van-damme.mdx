## Project Brief

We created a comprehensive story piece for Marie-France Van Damme, capturing the essence of her creative process and the atmosphere of her atelier. The project included professional voice-over recording to narrate her journey and artistic vision.

## Approach

- **Multi-Day Pre-Production**: Extensive planning to understand Marie-France's workflow and creative process.
- **Intimate Documentation**: Filmed various parts of the atelier, showcasing the environment where creativity happens.
- **Professional Voice-Over**: Recorded high-quality narration to complement the visual storytelling.
- **Cinematic Coverage**: Captured Marie-France walking through her workspace and creating her pieces.

## Result

A compelling brand story that authentically represents Marie-France Van Damme's artistic journey and creative environment, providing an intimate look into her atelier and design process.
