## Project Brief

We produced a concise 40-second brand story that connects Marie-France Van Damme to the vibrant culture of Long Street. The project captures the energy and creativity that inspires the designer's work.

## Approach

**Cultural Connection**: Highlighted the relationship between location and creative inspiration.
- **Concise Storytelling**: Delivered maximum impact within a 40-second timeframe.
- **Street Energy**: Captured the dynamic atmosphere and creative energy of Long Street.

## Result

A powerful short-form brand story that effectively connects the designer to her creative environment and cultural inspirations.
