## Project Brief

We documented Marie-France Van Damme's Peak collection launch, capturing the designer's creative process and the collection's unique aesthetic. The project provides an intimate look at high fashion creation.

## Approach

**Fashion Focus**: Emphasized the craftsmanship and attention to detail in each piece.
- **Designer Perspective**: Captured Marie-France's personal insights and creative philosophy.
- **Luxury Aesthetic**: Used sophisticated cinematography to match the collection's premium positioning.

## Result

An elegant fashion film that authentically represents the Peak collection and strengthens the brand's luxury positioning.
