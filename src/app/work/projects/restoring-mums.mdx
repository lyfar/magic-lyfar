## Project Brief

We collaborated with Restoring Mums, a specialized wellness company focused on postpartum massage treatments, to create a compelling brand story. The video showcases their complete treatment experience, from package delivery to the therapeutic session itself.

## Approach

- **Studio Design**: Organized and set up a studio space designed to look like a comfortable home apartment.
- **Professional Lighting**: Implemented comprehensive lighting setup to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
- **Complete Journey**: Filmed the entire client experience, including package arrival and treatment process.
- **Authentic Environment**: Created a realistic setting that reflects the home-visit nature of their services.

## Result

A professional brand video that effectively communicates Restoring Mums' specialized postpartum care services, demonstrating their commitment to providing comfortable, professional wellness treatments for new mothers in a home-like environment.
