## Project Brief

We filmed an in-depth interview with Robert Wilkins, Director Asia Pacific Corporate at Chanel Limited, as part of a series featuring C-level executives and directors sharing their professional experiences and insights within the luxury industry.

## Approach

- **Executive Interview Format**: Structured professional interview setting appropriate for senior leadership.
- **Professional Audio**: High-quality voice recording to capture clear, articulate responses.
- **Complete Production Cycle**: Full pre-production planning, professional filming, and post-production.
- **Leadership Series**: Part of a broader initiative documenting executive perspectives across the organization.

## Result

A polished executive interview that captures Robert Wilkins' expertise and leadership perspective, contributing to Chanel's internal leadership documentation and knowledge sharing across the Asia Pacific region.
