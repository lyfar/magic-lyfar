## Project Brief

We created a mobile-first vertical video designed for optimal engagement across social media platforms. The project focused on delivering impactful content within the constraints of vertical format storytelling.

## Approach

**Mobile Optimization**: Designed specifically for vertical viewing and social media consumption.
- **Quick Pacing**: Maintained high energy and quick cuts to capture and hold attention.
- **Visual Impact**: Used bold graphics and dynamic movement to maximize engagement.

## Result

A highly engaging vertical video that performs excellently across mobile platforms and social media channels.
